About the Librarian

I have been a librarian for a little over a decade, and in that time I have become convinced of one thing: the right book at the right moment can change the architecture of a person's interior life. This is not hyperbole. I have watched it happen.

This shelf is where I keep the books that have done this for me — across three territories I return to again and again: the spiritual, the historical, and the scientific. They are not separate in my mind. The mystic and the physicist are asking the same question. The Roman senator and the wandering pilgrim are navigating the same darkness.

Come in. Stay as long as you like.
