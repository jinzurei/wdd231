# Site Plan: Salt Lake Chamber

## Site Name
Salt Lake Chamber

## Site Purpose
Central online hub for Salt Lake City businesses and residents. Includes member directory, events, membership info, local weather, and tools to connect people and support local shopping.

## Target Audience
- Small business owners & entrepreneurs
- Corporate professionals
- New residents & visitors
- Community members

## Site Goals
- Give quick value (weather, events, featured businesses).
- Promote membership and directory use.
- Create a warm, easy mobile experience.

## User Personas
- **Alex Rivera** – 38, brewery owner. Needs weather, featured businesses, and events.
- **Jordan Patel** – 45, tech manager relocating. Wants directory and membership info.
- **Elena Kim** – 31, Laywer. Happy to defend and win your case.

## Scenarios
- Alex checks mobile for weather + events + businesses.
- Jordan browses business cards on phone and goes to Directory.
- Elena plans on desktop using hero CTA, weather, and events.

## SEO & Analytics
- **Keywords**: “Salt Lake Chamber events,” “SLC business directory,” “Salt Lake City networking.”
- **Track with Google Analytics**: CTA clicks, weather views, mobile use.

## Design Brief (Tuscan Sunset Palette)
Warm, inviting colors inspired by Tuscan sunsets.

- **Primary/Accent**: #E35336 (Deep Terracotta) – buttons, headers
- **Secondary**: #FFD3AC (Soft Peach) – cards, backgrounds
- **Mid-tone**: #9988A1 (Muted Lavender) – borders, text
- **Dark**: #8A2B0E (Deep Brown) – footer, nav
- **Background**: Light off-white/peach
