NP Membership

No Fee — Non-Profit Organizations

  • Basic listing in the online member directory
  • Monthly Chamber newsletter subscription
  • Invitations to community networking events
  • Access to Chamber resources and business guides
  • Voting rights in Chamber elections

Bronze Membership

$150 / year

  • All NP Membership benefits
  • Business listing with logo in the directory
  • 10% discount on Chamber-hosted events
  • Access to members-only training workshops
  • Ribbon-cutting ceremony for new businesses

Silver Membership

$350 / year

  • All Bronze Membership benefits
  • Annual spotlight feature in Chamber newsletter
  • 20% discount on all Chamber events
  • Four social media mentions per year
  • Priority registration for workshops & seminars

Gold Membership

$600 / year

  • All Silver Membership benefits
  • Quarterly spotlight on the Chamber homepage
  • 30% discount on all Chamber events
  • Free full-page ad in the annual Chamber magazine
  • Premium “Gold Member” badge in directory & search results
  • Exclusive invitations to Gold Member networking dinners
  • Complimentary booth at the annual Chamber Expo
